Facts about Moneta


  1. How the Town was started

    2. Three men bought 20 acres of land and along this branch was where the city of Moneta developed.


  2. Where name came from

    3. Mildred Thaxton suggest the name of Moneta from a book she was reading at the time, the book was either John Keats' "The Fall of Hyperion: A Dream [1856]," or Moneta, a subject of Roman Mythology.


  3. Railroad

    4. The building of the Virginian Railroad in the early 1900s played a big role in helping with putting Moneta on the map.


  4. Smith Mountain Dam

    5. Building of the Smith Mountain Dam provided over 600 jobs for the people of Moneta as well as over 500 miles of new shoreline which brought tourists.
