Facts about Moneta


  • How the Town was started

    • Three men bought 20 acres of land and along this branch was where the city of Moneta developed.


  • Where name came from

    • Mildred Thaxton suggest the name of Moneta from a book she was reading at the time, the book was either John Keats' "The Fall of Hyperion: A Dream [1856]," or Moneta, a subject of Roman Mythology.


  • Railroad

    • The building of the Virginian Railroad in the early 1900s played a big role in helping with putting Moneta on the map.


  • Smith Mountain Dam

    • Building of the Smith Mountain Dam provided over 600 jobs for the people of Moneta as well as over 500 miles of new shoreline which brought tourists.
