Facts about Richmond


  1. Man Made

    2. George Washington helped in designing of the James River and Kanawha Canal. The canal boatmen is represented by the figure in the center of the city flag.


  2. Canal Power

    3. Richmond emerged as an important industrial center after the American Revolutionary War because of the canal and hydropower generated from the falls.


  3. Speech

    4. Patrick Henry delivered his famous "Give me liberty, or give me death" speech in Richmond's St. John's Church in 1775.


  4. 20th Century

    5. The beginning of the 20th century, Richmond's population had reached 85,050 in 5sq mi making it the most densely populated city in the southern United States.
